GiveEdu Vocab

Welcome

Are you looking to improve your english? Do you want to increase your vocabulary?

GiveEdu Vocab is a mobile web application that will allow you to learn and practice new vocabulary with multiple choice questions. These vocabulary questions have different difficulty levels, which can be found on the study page. The range of words used in the app stretches across many different topics, such as colors, transportation, business, technology, and much more. For more information about GiveEdu, visit the about page.

How to Use

In order to use the app, select a multiple choice answer and push "Check Answer". You will be notified near the top of the screen if your answer was correct or not. If you got the question wrong, don't worry. The questions will eventually appear again as you cycle through and answer more. The questions are intended to connect and link meanings of different words together. This app is great for learning by association, you learn new words by relating them to words you already know.

Difficulty

You can select the difficulty of the questions in the drop down. Questions with simple sentence structure and subjects have a lower level, while questions involving more advanced vocabulary will be higher in level. Pick the level that is best suited toward your own educational needs.

About

This page contains details about our organization, the app, and more.

The Organization

GiveEdu is a NGO (Non-governmental Organization) devoted to providing highly accessible education to impoverished areas through online and mobile devices. GiveEdu is dedicated to creating and writing applications that make study materials and learning accessible to any person in any area of the world. GiveEdu is an international organization, which produces and distributes educational apps around the world via the web.

Our other goal is to develop new technologies and software that facilitates teaching for countries and areas of the world lacking the infrastructure to provide effective courses and materials. Our code is entirely opensource, and we believe the opensource model of software and the opensource community has the power to create incredible solutions in education around the globe. We want to build collaboration between engineers and teachers to transform education for those in need.

The App

This app provides a highly available environment to learn and improve english vocabulary. It is designed to work on almost any mobile browser, with minimal amounts of web content to load. This application is also entirely client side and can work offline. You only need to visit the site once to load it and study as much as you like.

Bugs and Improvements

To report a bug or glitch in the app, or to suggest an improvement, open an issue on our github repository. If you are interested in contributing questions to the app, you can submit a pull request here.

Leadership

GiveEdu was founded by Joshua Weinstein. Joshua is a software engineer living in the United States, near San Francisco. As an organization, we hope to expand ourselves to many more individuals and locations.
