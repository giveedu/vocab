Welcome

Are you looking to improve your english? Do you want to increase your vocabulary?

GiveEdu Vocab is a mobile web application that will allow you to learn and practice new vocabulary with multiple choice questions. These vocabulary questions have different difficulty levels, which can be found on the study page. For more information about GiveEdu, visit the about page.

About

GiveEdu is a NGO (Non-governmental Organization) devoted to providing highly accessible education to impoverished areas through online and mobile devices. GiveEdu is dedicated to creating and writing applications that make study materials and learning accessible to any person in any area of the world.
